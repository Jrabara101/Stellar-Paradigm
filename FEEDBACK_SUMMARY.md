## Themes & Next Steps

1. **Word Hint confusion (priority)** — Dayniel Talusig flagged the "word hint" feature as an issue, and is the *only* respondent who said he would not play again. These two data points line up, making this the single highest-priority fix to investigate.
2. **Definition coverage** — Darid De Jesus noted that not all words have a definition shown as a hint. Worth auditing the word list for gaps.
3. **Board mechanic feedback** — Ricky Mark Mercado gave detailed feedback that the board "resets" after each word instead of building continuously like a real Scrabble board, and suggested a bigger, persistent board. Darid De Jesus separately asked for a "bigger Scrabble-style board" and a play-to-earn mode. This independently validates the existing Scrabble-mode item already on the roadmap.
4. **Difficulty/variety requests** — Cedrick Cadence Cornejo asked for more difficulty options.

## Bottom Line

9/10 real testers said they'd play again, with a 4.9/5 average rating and no reports of broken core functionality — the feedback collected skews toward feature requests and polish, not bugs.
