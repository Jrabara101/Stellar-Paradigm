How to Play

  • 1
    Solve the Scramble: Drag-and-drop or tap tiles to place them on the highlighted grid slots.
  • 2
    Get Hints: Click Word Hint for a definition, or Hint to lock a correct tile (costs 10 pts).
  • 3
    Submit: Complete the word and click Submit to advance levels! Build a Win Streak for bonus points: +10 (1–5), +20 (6–10), +30 (11–15), +50 (16+).

Hint Guide

  • 1
    First Letter Only: The hint only reveals and locks the first letter of the word to keep the scramble challenging.
  • 2
    Cost & Logic: Each hint costs 10 points. If the word is wrong, the hint stays on the board.
  • 3
    Persistence: Locked hints will not return to the rack on a Reset or wrong submission.

Game Updates

v1.7.0 - Hybrid Save System

  • Address-Keyed Autosave: In-progress game state (score, level, category, streak) is now saved to localStorage under your wallet address after every correct answer — no fee, instant.
  • Session Resume: Reconnecting your wallet automatically restores your last in-progress level and score.
  • On-Chain Source of Truth: Your all-time best score remains on the Soroban leaderboard; the personal best panel now uses the on-chain entry as an authoritative baseline.
  • No Wallet, No Problem: Anonymous players still get local best-score tracking under a shared anonymous key.

v1.6.1 - Sound Icon Alignment

  • Sound Toggle Offset: Adjusted the fixed mute button down to top: 48px, ensuring it is no longer covered by the Stellar Wallet bar.

v1.6.0 - Atomic Leaderboards & Personal Best Tracker

  • On-Chain Leaderboard UI: View and fetch real-time player high scores directly from the Stellar Testnet (Soroban) smart contract.
  • Personal Best Storage: Saves your highest score and maximum level locally using browser localStorage, integrating seamlessly with your connected on-chain identity.
  • Mock Fallback Mechanics: Provides high-fidelity local high score rankings if the blockchain ledger is unreachable or empty.
  • Metallic Rank Badges: Styled custom gold, silver, and bronze badges to highlight the top three leaderboard performers.

v1.5.0 - Custom Chips, Background Music & Alignment Fixes

  • 8 Skeuomorphic Chip Materials: Customize your tiles via the new "Customize Chips" menu. Choose from Bone, Acrylic, Aluminum, Mahogany, Ebony, Vibrant Amber, Malachite, and Smoked Glass!
  • Dynamic Jazz Loop: A relaxing, dynamic bossa nova loop synthesized directly in your browser using the Web Audio API, controllable via the mute toggle.
  • Tile Alignment Fix: Absolute coordinates are now calculated relative to the main body element, resolving the drift/alignment bug of letters on the board.
  • Layout Stability: Fixed the clue panel height to prevent layout shifts when clues change or grow.

v1.4.0 - Progressive Clue & Letter Lock System

  • 3-Stage Clue System: Definitions are now active by default as Clue 1. Progress to Clue 2 (first letter lock) and Clue 3 (Trump Card - first & last letter lock) on wrong guesses or hint clicks.
  • Retro HUD Indicator Dots: 3 visual glow indicators (green, amber, red/gold with pulse) show active clue levels in the monitored HUD panel.
  • Point/Streak Mechanics: Requesting hints costs 10 pts but preserves win streaks. Wrong guesses lock letters for free but reset streaks.
  • Definition Toggle: Clicking "Word Hint" toggles the display back to the definition at any time.

v1.3.0 - Customizable Board & Wallpaper Themes

  • Custom Board Themes: Open the "Customize Board" menu in the header to pick between 5 stunning aesthetics: Mid-Century Teak, Nordic Oak, Rustic Reclaimed, Vintage Parchment, and Coastal Driftwood.
  • Complete Theme Overrides: Overrides page background wallpapers, board designs, grid cells, locked tiles, and button finishes to match the theme.
  • Image Previews: Preview cards with screenshots to preview the design before activating.
  • Persistent Styling: The custom board choice is saved in local browser storage to persist across sessions!

v1.2.0 - Win Streak Update

  • Win Streak Counter: A new Streak bubble in the header tracks your consecutive correct answers.
  • Tiered Streak Bonuses: Win multiple rounds without a mistake to earn bonus points per solve — +10 (1–5), +20 (6–10), +30 (11–15), +50 (16+ wins).
  • Score Breakdown: The victory screen now shows a detailed breakdown of base points, streak tier, and total earned per round.
  • Streak Reset: A wrong answer resets your streak. Switching category also resets it.

v1.1.0 - Challenge & Hints Update

  • First Letter Hint Lock: The Hint button now only reveals and locks the first letter to maintain a healthy puzzle challenge.
  • Persistent Hints: Correctly placed hints stay locked on the board even if you submit an incorrect word or press Reset.
  • Hint Guide Sidebar: A new desktop sidebar detailing point costs and hint rules.
  • Interactive Changelog: Added this updates log to keep players informed of new features!

v1.0.0 - Teak Cabinet Launch

  • Retro Teak Aesthetic: Beautiful Mid-Century Modern graphics with starburst pattern and brass accents.
  • Web Audio Synthesizer: Immersive retro sounds generated dynamically in your browser.
  • Canvas Particle Burst: Spectacular physics explosions on victory.
  • Categories & APIs: Wikipedia and Dictionary APIs feed categories like Anime, Novels, and Filipino Movies.
