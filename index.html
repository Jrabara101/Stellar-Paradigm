How to Play

  • 1
    Solve the Scramble: Drag-and-drop or tap tiles to place them on the highlighted grid slots.
  • 2
    Get Hints: Click Definition (free) to reveal the word's meaning, or Hint to lock a correct tile (costs 10 pts).
  • 3
    Submit: Complete the word and click Submit to advance levels! Build a Win Streak for bonus points: +10 (1–5), +20 (6–10), +30 (11–15), +50 (16+).

Hint Guide

  • 1
    First Letter Only: The hint only reveals and locks the first letter of the word to keep the scramble challenging.
  • 2
    Cost & Logic: Each hint costs 10 points. If the word is wrong, the hint stays on the board.
  • 3
    Persistence: Locked hints will not return to the rack on a Reset or wrong submission.

Game Updates

v1.11.0 - Autumn Theme, New Chips & Smarter Feedback

  • Autumn Harvest Theme: A warm burnt-orange board with amber tiles joins the theme collection.
  • New Chip Materials: Brass, Jade, and Mother-of-Pearl chips are now available in the Customize Chips menu.
  • Bigger Daily Challenge: The daily word pool grew from 33 to over 1,100 curated words — years of dailies with no repeats.
  • Near-Miss Feedback: Wrong guesses now flash correctly-placed letters amber (with a gentler sound) so you know what to keep.
  • Golden Cascade: Solving a word sends a golden ripple across the board cells under the bouncing tiles.
  • Score Odometer: Points now count up with a satisfying pulse instead of snapping to the new total.
  • Streak Glow Tiers: The streak bubble now glows mustard at 3 wins, orange at 7, and catches fire at 10.
  • New Sounds: Combo blips that rise with your multiplier, an achievement fanfare, and a daily-streak bell.
  • Offline Fix: The Rustic theme no longer depends on remote images — every theme now works fully offline.

v1.10.0 - Visual Polish, Achievements & Combo Scoring

  • Tile Settle Physics: Tiles now bounce gently into place when dropped onto the board or rack.
  • Premium Tile Effects: Volcanic Forge, Holographic, Ice Crystal, and Stained Glass tile effects are now unlockable for players holding a GOLD or LEGEND badge — None and Neon Arcade stay free.
  • Shareable Victory Card: Turn any win — regular or Daily Challenge — into a shareable image with your word, score, streak, and badge tier.
  • Ambient Backgrounds: Each board theme now has a subtle drifting glow behind the board (respects Performance Mode and reduced-motion settings).
  • Achievements: Earn one-time achievements for a no-hint solve and a sub-10-second solve, shown in the victory score breakdown.
  • Combo Multiplier: Win two scrambles within 60 seconds of each other to build up to a 3x combo on your base score.

v1.9.0 - Daily Challenge, Performance Mode & Onboarding

  • Daily Challenge: A new word every day, the same for every player — accessible from ☰ Menu. Solve it, then share your result with a Wordle-style emoji grid showing how many hints you needed.
  • Daily Streak: Come back each day to build a streak, shown right in the header while a Daily Challenge is active.
  • Performance Mode: A new toggle in the Effects menu strips heavy shadows and blur for smoother play on slower devices.
  • Welcome Onboarding: First-time visitors now see a quick "how to play" card explaining drag-and-drop and that a wallet is optional — connect anytime to save your score on-chain.

v1.8.2 - Tile Font Picker

  • Tile Font Picker: Choose your letter tile font in the Customize Chips menu — Classic (Jost), Typewriter (Courier Prime), Grotesk (Space Grotesk), and Modern Bold (Montserrat) are free and available immediately.
  • Easy Read Font: Added OpenDyslexic as an accessibility-focused tile font option.
  • Premium Fonts: Arcade (Bungee) and Retro Pixel (Press Start 2P) are unlockable tile fonts for players holding a GOLD or LEGEND badge on the on-chain leaderboard.
  • Lazy Font Loading: Accessibility and premium fonts only download when you open Customize Chips or have one already selected — the initial page load stays untouched.

v1.8.1 - Speed & Performance Update

  • Installable App (PWA): Word Scramble can now be installed to your home screen or desktop, with instant repeat loads and an offline game shell via a service worker.
  • Smarter Live Updates: The on-chain event stream now polls every 5s only while the tab is visible, slows to 30s in background tabs, catches up instantly when you return, and stays fully paused until a wallet connects.
  • Leaderboard Cache: Leaderboard reads are cached for 15 seconds and refresh automatically the moment any player submits a new score — reopening the leaderboard is now instant.
  • Faster Wallet Connect: The connection to the wallet SDK's CDN is pre-warmed at page load, so the first "Connect Wallet" click resolves quicker.

v1.8.0 - Interface Overhaul & Performance Update

  • Header Menu Dropdown: The five header buttons (Category, Customize Board, Customize Chips, Leaderboard, Effects) are now collapsed into a single ☰ Menu dropdown on all screen sizes, freeing up space above the board.
  • Title & Navbar Fix: The page now grows and scrolls instead of clipping — the Word Scramble title no longer hides underneath the fixed wallet bar on any screen size.
  • Clue Panel Overflow Fix: Long dictionary definitions wrap inside the clue card (matched to the board width) and scroll internally past 150px, instead of stretching the whole board sideways.
  • Letter Rack Relocated: The tile rack moved from the bottom-right corner to sit centered directly below the board, with tiles enlarged from 38px to 52px (and bigger letters on mobile too).
  • Readable Side Panels: "How to Play" and "Hint Guide" cards widened to 340px with larger text (0.95rem), bigger headings, and larger step badges for readability.
  • Smoother Touch & Drag: Touch listeners now attach only while dragging a tile (fixing a page-wide scroll-blocking violation), and drag snap-targets are measured once per drag instead of on every mouse move.
  • Leaner Deployment: ~87MB of non-runtime files (videos, documents, screenshots) are excluded from the live site deploy.

v1.7.0 - Hybrid Save System

  • Address-Keyed Autosave: In-progress game state (score, level, category, streak) is now saved to localStorage under your wallet address after every correct answer — no fee, instant.
  • Session Resume: Reconnecting your wallet automatically restores your last in-progress level and score.
  • On-Chain Source of Truth: Your all-time best score remains on the Soroban leaderboard; the personal best panel now uses the on-chain entry as an authoritative baseline.
  • No Wallet, No Problem: Anonymous players still get local best-score tracking under a shared anonymous key.

v1.6.1 - Sound Icon Alignment

  • Sound Toggle Offset: Adjusted the fixed mute button down to top: 48px, ensuring it is no longer covered by the Stellar Wallet bar.

v1.6.0 - Atomic Leaderboards & Personal Best Tracker

  • On-Chain Leaderboard UI: View and fetch real-time player high scores directly from the Stellar Testnet (Soroban) smart contract.
  • Personal Best Storage: Saves your highest score and maximum level locally using browser localStorage, integrating seamlessly with your connected on-chain identity.
  • Mock Fallback Mechanics: Provides high-fidelity local high score rankings if the blockchain ledger is unreachable or empty.
  • Metallic Rank Badges: Styled custom gold, silver, and bronze badges to highlight the top three leaderboard performers.

  • 8 Skeuomorphic Chip Materials: Customize your tiles via the new "Customize Chips" menu. Choose from Bone, Acrylic, Aluminum, Mahogany, Ebony, Vibrant Amber, Malachite, and Smoked Glass!
  • Dynamic Jazz Loop: A relaxing, dynamic bossa nova loop synthesized directly in your browser using the Web Audio API, controllable via the mute toggle.
  • Tile Alignment Fix: Absolute coordinates are now calculated relative to the main body element, resolving the drift/alignment bug of letters on the board.
  • Layout Stability: Fixed the clue panel height to prevent layout shifts when clues change or grow.

v1.4.0 - Progressive Clue & Letter Lock System

  • 3-Stage Clue System: Definitions are now active by default as Clue 1. Progress to Clue 2 (first letter lock) and Clue 3 (Trump Card - first & last letter lock) on wrong guesses or hint clicks.
  • Retro HUD Indicator Dots: 3 visual glow indicators (green, amber, red/gold with pulse) show active clue levels in the monitored HUD panel.
  • Point/Streak Mechanics: Requesting hints costs 10 pts but preserves win streaks. Wrong guesses lock letters for free but reset streaks.
  • Definition Toggle: Clicking "Word Hint" toggles the display back to the definition at any time.

  • Image Previews: Preview cards with screenshots to preview the design before activating.
  • Persistent Styling: The custom board choice is saved in local browser storage to persist across sessions!

  • Score Breakdown: The victory screen now shows a detailed breakdown of base points, streak tier, and total earned per round.
